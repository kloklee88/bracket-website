Simkung

Lovable bracket game for your favorite stuff!

Get Started

Brackets are life, brackets are love!

Simkung (심쿵) is like a type of emotional heart attack you feel when you see or think about your crush or someone you find extremely attractive! Remember it by knowing that 심장 (simjang) means “heart” and 쿵쿵 (kungkung) is the “thud” or “booming” sound your heartbeat makes!

How it works

You will be first be given options on what data, bracket size, and group filters to start off your bracket. Afterwards, you then are given rounds of head to head choices. You continue until you get to the winner!
