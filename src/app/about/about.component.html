About

The developer was bored and wanted to make something with Angular. This website is the product! Hopefully you enjoy your stay!

This application is to have some fun and determine your favorite song, group, or anything in a randomnly generated tournament bracket.

You will be first be given options on what data, bracket size, and group filters to start off your bracket. Afterwards, you then are given rounds of head to head choices. You continue until you get to the winner!

Click on "Start Bracket" to get started!
